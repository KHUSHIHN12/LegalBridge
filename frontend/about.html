What is LegalBridge

A modern legal research companion for Indian criminal law.

LegalBridge simplifies a real legal transition challenge: understanding how established IPC references correspond to the Bharatiya Nyaya Sanhita framework. Instead of forcing users to manually cross-reference provisions, LegalBridge organizes that intelligence into a searchable, guided platform.

The result is a practical legal-tech layer for faster case review, educational exploration, and structured analysis of criminal complaint descriptions using mapped statutory references.
